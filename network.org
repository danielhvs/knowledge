* Network
** check which process is using a given `port`:

#+begin_src sh
netstat -anpe | grep "8010" | grep "LISTEN"
#+end_src

** get it's pid:
#+begin_src sh
netstat -anpe | grep "8010" | grep "LISTEN" | awk '{print $NF}' | cut -d'/' -f1
#+end_src

** kill it already:
#+begin_src sh
netstat -anpe | grep "8010" | grep "LISTEN" | awk '{print $NF}' | cut -d'/' -f1 | xargs kill
#+end_src
