* Add other custom Repositories in nexus3:

1. Go to admin page -> Settings -> Repositories -> Repositories
1. Click `+Create Repository`
1. Click “Create repository” -> choose recipe “maven2 (proxy)”.
1. Fill in at least:
1.1 Name: clojars (or similar).
1.1 Remote storage URL: https://clojars.org/repo/.
1.1 Version: Mixed
1. Leave other defaults as‑is and click “Create repository”.
1. This tells Nexus how to reach Clojars as an upstream Maven repository.
1. Add the custom repo to the maven-public group: go to maven-public and at the bottom add it then click `save`
